Scrambled Eggs

Egg are one of my favourite food, Here is an recipe for deliciously Scrambled Eggs

Inggridients

  1. 2 eggs
  2. 1tbs butter
  3. 2tbs cream

Method

  • Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  • Gently mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
  • Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
